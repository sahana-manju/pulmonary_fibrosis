About

Pulmonary fibrosis is a condition in which the lungs become scarred over time.Symptoms include shortness of breadth,dry cough and fatigue.Treatments for it is directed towards improving the symptoms.Medications may be used to slow the worsening of scarring.

Clinicians find it very difficult to diagnose and predict prognosis of the disease even with the help of CT scan.This system provides a way to assist clinicians to predict the prognosis of the disease based on the Forced Vital Capacity (FVC) and plan optimal treatment options.
